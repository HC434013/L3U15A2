Careers

Are you ready to join a team that's as passionate about web development as you are?

At Hot Beans Web, we're always on the lookout for talented individuals who are eager to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world.

Before you apply...

We need to ensure you're qualified to apply with us. Please note our applicant requirements below.

Qualifications:
- At least one year of post-secondary education in a sector related to web development, computer science or IT
- Completion of a web development training course provided by one of our partners*

Skills:
- Proficiency in either frontend or backend development, full-stack is preferred but still optional



*Partners include Codecademy, Udemy, and Coursera; please contact us for further inquiries in regards to accepted courses

What to Expect

We work 100% remote internally and with our clients, so a good quality internet connection and computer is essential.

Punctuality for at least one online meeting per day is expected, working hours tend to span around noon to the early evening.

How to Apply

Think you've got the chops to join us? 
Then fill out our contact form and we'll get back to you in 3-5 working days!
